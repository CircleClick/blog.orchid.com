Orchid is a habitat for software development modeled on mutuality.

Like the give and take of the natural world, the relationship between developers and users is mutually beneficial. With Orchid, developers finally have the ideal conditions to create a natural internet that can scale seamlessly through direct relationships and efficient infrastructure.

Features of Orchid

  • Adaptability

    Developers can build the internet they want to build—without worrying about shouldering the cost of the infrastructure. Scaling is seamless because every user contributes to the network’s shared resources.

  • Harmony

    Orchid expands the reach of developers by welcoming users from around the world. Developers can build relationships directly with users, who have exclusive control over their privacy and identity, creating a secure network of connected autonomy.

  • Resilience

    Because the network is distributed efficiently across user-initiated servers, whatever is built with Orchid lasts as long as it’s needed or wanted.
