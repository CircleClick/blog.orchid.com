Set-up Guide

To start using Orchid, you need an Orchid Account. This set-up guide will show you how to create an Orchid Account and connect that account to the Orchid app using an Ethereum wallet and Orchid’s digital currency: OXT.

If you already have a QR code for an existing Orchid account, skip to step 4 and simply open the app to scan/paste in the code and add the account. Your VPN service will then be paid for from that account.

1

Create a new Ethereum wallet address

Privacy note: we recommend using a new Ethereum wallet for Orchid that is not linked to other Ethereum products or services you use. (learn more)

2

Add OXT

Add at least $4 USD of OXT and $1 USD of ETH into your web3 browser.

  • $4 of OXT is estimated to provide 60 GBs of VPN service
  • $1 of ETH should be enough for 2-3 transactions (learn more)

We recommend buying OXT using a large exchange for transaction privacy (learn more). Buy OXT and ETH right now on Coinbase.

3

Create your Orchid account

Load account.orchid.com in your wallet’s browser. It is a good idea to bookmark account.orchid.com. Read the directions on the screen and add any OXT or ETH that you need to create the account. Tap the “Create account” button and then confirm two prompts from your wallet to move the OXT into the Orchid account.

Once complete, scan or copy the Orchid account to your clipboard for step 4

4

Add your account to the Orchid app

Download the Orchid app for Android. Help us beta test our app on iOS TestFlight

Open the app and tap "New Hop". Scan or paste in the Orchid account. The app will automatically use that account to generate a hop, and the app will be ready.

5

Start exploring freely!

On the Orchid app’s main screen, switch the toggle in the top right to “on” to enable Orchid on your device and activate your configured hops. That’s it! You’re ready to start exploring freely.
Stay curious out there!

Note: If you notice your Internet connection stops after turning Orchid on, simply turn it off and back on again to recycle the connection and connect to a different server.
