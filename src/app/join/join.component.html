Set-up Guide

To start using Orchid, you need an Orchid Account. This set-up guide will show you how to create an Orchid Account and connect that account to the Orchid app using an Ethereum wallet and Orchid’s digital currency: OXT.

If you already have a QR code for an existing Orchid account, Play from step 4 and simply open the app to scan/paste in the code and add the account. Your VPN service will then be paid for from that account.

Check out Coinbase’s Earn Campaign for an opportunity to earn OXT while learning about Orchid!

Step 1

Create a new Ethereum wallet

Play from: 01:06

We recommend using Metamask for your Ethereum wallet.

Alternatively, Trust Wallet, BRD Wallet and Coinbase Wallet are also compatible with Orchid.

Privacy note: We recommend using a new Ethereum wallet address for Orchid that is not linked to other Ethereum products or services you use.

Step 3

Create your Orchid Account

Play from: 05:38

Load account.orchid.com in your wallet’s browser. It’s a good idea to bookmark this page for the future.

Read the directions on the screen and add the OXT or ETH needed to create the account.

Tap the
Create Account
button and then confirm two prompts from your wallet to move the OXT into your Orchid account.

Once complete, scan or copy your Orchid Account to your clipboard.

Step 4

Connect your Account and app

Play from: 07:21

Download the Orchid app for Android and help us Beta test our app on iOS Testflight.

Open the app and scan or paste in your Orchid Account. The app will automatically use that Account to generate a configured hop, and the app will be ready use!

Step 5

Start exploring freely!

Play from: 10:44

On the Orchid app’s main screen, switch the toggle in the top right to “on”. This enables Orchid on your device and activates your configured hops.

That’s it! You’re ready to start exploring freely.
Stay curious out there!

Note: If you notice your Internet connection stops after turning Orchid on, simply turn it off and back on again to recycle the connection and connect to a different server.
