Episode 3: Jyri Engestrom - June 17th

In our third episode, we go down the rabbit hole with Jyri Engestrom, a partner at Yes VC, a venture capital firm that invests in the pre-seed and seed stages of companies set in motion by social movements. He discusses his journey as a founder of both Jaiku and Ditto, which were later acquired separately by Google and Groupon. He also shares his thoughts on the future of democracy, what we can do to protect our privacy online, and some interesting efforts he and a few other entrepreneurs are undertaking to provide testing for COVID19.

Episode 1: Interview Rich Staropoli - Premiere June 3rd

In our premiere episode, we go down the rabbit hole with Rich Staropoli, whose career reads like a Tom Clancy spy novel. He's the former White House CIO for the Department of Homeland Security and a special agent for the Secret Service for 25 years. He discusses what it was like to protect President George W. Bush on September 11th, his thoughts on the current state of technology at DHS, what cybersecurity risks our country faces, and the future of our election systems.
