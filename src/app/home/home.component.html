Orchid is the natural internet.

As humans, we naturally crave agency and free will. When we see the Internet as an extension of our nature, we strive to keep it open and protect it, so that everyone can collaborate and access opportunities across the globe.

Orchid is a set of open-source tools built and run by people who want the Internet to be an extension of our nature. Our initial implementation is a network built on Ethereum and a VPN client for all major operating systems.
