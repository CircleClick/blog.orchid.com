Our approach

We believe that by harnessing the power of blockchain, we can offer users a better VPN experience.

  • Pay-per-use

    On Orchid, you only pay for service while actually using the VPN. Turn it off, and there are no charges. And no monthly fees!

  • Provider network

    Change providers within the Orchid network with a click. If you don't like one, simply tap to reconnect and get randomly reassigned.

  • Unlimited devices

    You can share your account with an unlimited number of devices and people. Manage one account for your friends, family and all their devices.
