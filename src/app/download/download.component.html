Try the pre-release Orchid app

iOS

  1. Tap the link below on your iOS device.
    https://www.orchid.com/ios-pre-release
  2. Follow the instruction to install TestFlight.
  3. Install Orchid.

Android

  1. Tap the link below on your Android device.
    https://www.orchid.com/android-pre-release
  2. Tap the "Join group" link to join the Android Testers Google group.
  3. Once you have joined the google group, download the app here: https://play.google.com/apps/testing/net.orchid
  4. Install Orchid.
