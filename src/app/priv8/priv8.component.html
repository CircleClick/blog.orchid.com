ABOUT

What is happening to privacy?

“Privacy is important.” We hear that platitude from media, government, and companies. But what does privacy mean in a world reeling from a global pandemic, economic disaster, police shootings and riots? During the chaos and confusion, leaders look to calm new fears at the cost of old freedoms. The ground is quietly shifting on privacy. Join us for an in-depth discussion on the state of privacy in today’s world with the foremost experts, policymakers, and builders.
